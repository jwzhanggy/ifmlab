Position Openings

IFM Lab has several open researcher positions.

3 PhD researcher positions available:
2 RA and 1 TA
starting from 2019 Spring or 2019 Fall.

Limited funding for postdoctoral researchers, but visiting postdoctoral researchers are very welcome.

Visiting scholar positions available for professors/students on data mining, machine learning, GIS, bioinformatics.

Several research positions are open to master and undergraduate students interested in data mining/machine learning.

PhD Researcher Positions (2 RA and 1 TA available)

IFM Lab Qualifications:

  • Candidates should have a quantitative background, with a master/bachelor degree in computer science, software engineering or related fields.
  • Strong interest in working in an highly interdisciplinary environment about data mining and machine learning.
  • Strong skills in algorithm design, programming, statistics and mathematics.
  • Proficiency in English academic paper reading, writing, and English communication.
  • Publication records at top-tier conferences/journals (Required for master applicants, Optional for undergraduate applicants).

CS Department Requirements: (International, Domestic )

  • Submit the Florida State University Online Application: https://admissions.fsu.edu/gradapp/ with the required attachments.
  • Undergraduate/Master degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or a degree in a related area with substantial coursework in Computer Science.
  • One official or certified of all transcript(s), diploma(s), and/or degree certificate(s) from every college or university attended.
  • Official GRE scores (Minimum Requirement: 145 on Verbal; 150 on Quantitative) submitted to institution code 5219 via ETS.
  • Official TOEFL (Minimum Requirement: Total 80) scores submitted to institution code 5219 via ETS (International Student Only).
  • 3 letters of recommendation.
  • Statement of Purpose, and Curriculum Vitae.
  • Application Processing Fee of $30.00.

How to Apply

  • Application deadlines: January 15th (International) and June 1st (Domestic) for the Fall Semester; September 1st (International) and November 1st (Domestic) for the Spring Semester.
  • Official application through the admission website of the CS department of Florida State University. The CS admission website URL is: http://www.cs.fsu.edu/admissions/graduate-admissions/.
  • Submit a copy of your application with your CV, statement of interest, and copies of two representative publications (if you have) via email to Prof. Jiawei Zhang (jwzhanggy@gmail.com), with subject "Application for PhD Researcher Position".
  • IFM Lab will start reviewing the applications 3 months before the application deadline and will continue until the positions are filled.

Postdoctoral Researcher Positions (visiting postdoctoral researcher positions available)

IFM Lab Qualifications:

  • Candidates should have a quantitative background, with a PhD degree in computer science, software engineering or related fields.
  • Strong interest in working in an highly interdisciplinary environment about data mining, machine learning, natural language processing, geographical information system, bioinformatics.
  • Strong skills in algorithm design, programming, statistics and mathematics.
  • Proficiency in English academic paper reading, writing, and English communication.
  • Outstanding publication records at top-tier conferences/journals in data mining, machine learning, AI, GIS, bioinformatics.
  • Independent and secure funding sources.

How to Apply

  • Normal starting date: August 5th (for Fall) or January 5th (for Spring) of each academic year. Please apply 5 months prior to the anticipated starting date.
  • Submit your application with cover letter, CV, research statement, three representative publications as a package via email (jwzhanggy@gmail.com) to Prof. Jiawei Zhang, with subject "Application for Postdoctoral Researcher Position".
  • Arrange for three letters of reference to be sent to Prof. Jiawei Zhang (jwzhanggy@gmail.com) via email within two weeks of submitting your application. Please indicate your name in email subject.
  • The review of applications will begin immediately and will continue until the positions are filled.
